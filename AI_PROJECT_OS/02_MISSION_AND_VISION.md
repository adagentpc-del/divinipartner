# 02 Mission and Vision

## Mission

Be the commerce and coordination infrastructure for events: let venues, vendors, planners, and clients discover each other, agree on scope and price, transact, and run the event, all in one place, with the platform taking a transparent cut of the money that flows through it.

The Pricing V2 repositioning frames the product publicly as "Event Commerce Infrastructure" rather than an "AI marketplace" or a "subscription tool." (Source: Divini-Partners-Pricing-V2-Plan.md.)

## Vision

- A marketplace where signup is free for every role, friction to transact is near zero, and the platform earns when value changes hands (a flat fee added on top of each booking) rather than gating access behind subscription tiers.
- Venues are incentivized to bring transactions on-platform because they earn a share of every booking they host.
- An intelligence layer (AI COO, Divini Score, playbooks, war room, relationship graph) that compounds: the more events run through the platform, the smarter its recommendations and the stickier it becomes.

## Strategic pillars

1. Transparent, on-top transaction economics (vendors made whole; client sees the fee; venue shares in it).
2. Free, frictionless onboarding for all roles (no tiers, no bid windows).
3. A compounding intelligence moat that turns event history into recommendations.
4. Trust and legal posture that lets the platform stay "not a party" to the underlying transaction while still earning a fee.

## Non-goals (current)

- Not a subscription-tier business (V2 removes tiers).
- Not a holder of funds. The intent is that funds settle to vendors (Stripe Connect) and the platform takes only an application fee. See `52_COMPLIANCE.md`.
