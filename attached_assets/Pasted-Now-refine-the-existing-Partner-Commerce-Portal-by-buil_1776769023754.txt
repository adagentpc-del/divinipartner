11. METRIC DEFINITIONS AND CONSISTENCY
-----------------------------------

Ensure core metrics are calculated consistently and clearly.

Examples:
- estimated gross margin = retail amount minus estimated supplier cost
- actual gross margin = retail amount minus final supplier cost
- commission variance = expected commission minus paid commission
- revenue at risk = revenue linked to blocked, overdue, or unresolved at-risk records
- forecasted revenue = booked revenue for upcoming events/orders within selected range
- open receivables = invoiced but unpaid balance

Show metric tooltips or small info helpers where useful.
Make definitions consistent across dashboard cards and tables.
